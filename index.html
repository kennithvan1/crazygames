Game Overview

Ragdoll Archers is an exciting online archery game where players test their aim and strategy. Take control of your archer, draw your bow, and hit targets with precision. With realistic physics and ragdoll effects, every shot creates a unique and entertaining outcome.

How to Play

  • Use your mouse to aim your bow and arrow
  • Click and hold to draw your bow, release to shoot
  • Adjust your aim to account for gravity and distance
  • Hit targets to score points and advance through levels
  • Compete with friends to see who can achieve the highest score
